Technical Department

The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning introduces the departmental society, "KRITRIM DHI". Kritrim Dhi has four branches KRITRIM R & D, KRITRIM TECH, KRITRIM EVENT MANAGEMENT AND SPORTS and KRITRIM SOCIAL MEDIA. Following are the main objectives of the Society.

Objectives

  • To make students aware and help them to learn various concepts of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Software development.
  • To ignite innovation within programmers and put an emphasis on the mirthful future that lies in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
  • To focus on innovation and study of various aspects and implementation of AI and Machine Learning.
  • To increase student participation in awarding events like Hackathons, Coding Competitions and Open Source.
